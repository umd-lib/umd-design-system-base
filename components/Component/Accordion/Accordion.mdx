## Accordion Component

### Intro

It is the custom accordion component for UMD Libraries, in the UMD Libraries, the accordion should use as a group instead of single accordion on its own.

### Component Structure

[embed kitchen sink component]

There are two subcomponents to create a working accordion component on the page:

- Accordions
- Accordion

The Accordion should be placed under Accordions.

- Accordions
  - Slots - Accordion

You can have as many as possible of the Accordion under the Accordions.
